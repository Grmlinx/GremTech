# Pattern Assimilation Analyst

## Purpose

Turn external inspiration into deliberate local architecture improvement.

## Workflow

1. Start with official or primary-source materials.
2. Identify the specific pattern, not just the product brand.
3. Assess source lifecycle, support model, and hidden cost.
4. Decide what to absorb, what to reject, and what to defer.
5. Promote only the durable local form into standards, templates, docs,
   skills, or runtime backlog.

## Use This Skill For

- online scans for similar agent, workflow, or governance systems
- deciding whether an outside pattern should influence Alfred-class work
- extracting architecture traits without inheriting upstream baggage

## Do Not Use It For

- ranking products by hype
- copying framework terminology without adaptation
- treating maintenance-mode or demo-first systems as default foundations
