# Absorb Traits Not Stacks

## Lesson

External inspiration is strongest when the local system absorbs the trait, not
the whole stack.

## Why It Matters

Agent frameworks and products often bundle useful ideas with lifecycle baggage,
cloud assumptions, support constraints, or architecture language that should
not become `GremTech` defaults.

## Reuse Rule

Future scans should:

1. assess source vitality and support posture
2. extract the concrete strengthening trait
3. reject the non-essential baggage
4. promote only the durable local form
