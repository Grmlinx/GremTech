# Explicit Release Posture

## Lesson

Mature repos do not stop at "ready" or "not ready."

They state exactly what is supported, unsupported, approval-required,
maintainer-required, and blocked.

## Why It Matters

Without explicit release posture, capability leaks outward through ambiguity:
users assume a feature is supported because it exists, or assume a boundary is
strong because it was implied somewhere in prose.

## Reuse Rule

Future governed repos should:

- publish explicit release or adoption posture
- point each posture claim at proof or decision evidence
- keep risk acceptance separate from support claims
